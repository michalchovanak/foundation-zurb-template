Our
facility

Research and development sits at the heart of everything we do at our facility in Voderady.

LEARN MORE

R&D Labs

Three laboratories, each equipped with the latest technology, give our scientists an industry-unique advantage to rapidly develop full commercial size battery prototypes, essential for capturing real-world data and eliminating risks associated with the transition from prototype to commercial scale.

Production

Initial integrated laboratory and semi-automatic pilot line is producing numerous different size pouch formats and chemistries. The pilot line will provide the foundation for a manufacturing production line capable of producing multiple GWh.

Team

Established in September 2019 and headquartered in Slovakia, InoBat Auto is led by a team with exceptional experience in the automotive, aerospace and finance industries.
