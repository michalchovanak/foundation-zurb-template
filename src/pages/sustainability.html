Our
philosophy

sustainability

At InoBat Auto, we focus our world-leadingR&D capabilities on creating the most efficient, best value batteries possible to help deliver a sustainable future for all. However, we are acutely aware that the end does not justifythe means.

The potential impact of battery production begins long before the first prototype has been built and extends long after the production unit has been delivered to customers. InoBat Auto takes full responsibility every step of the way through our Cradle to Cradle (C2C) business plan, which is firmly rooted in UN sustainability goals.

Our culture demands that a moral compass guide us for every decision we make – no excuses, no shortcuts, and no half measures. Before any material or component can be considered for inclusion in our whole life cycle plan, its supplier must successfully pass through scrupulous environmental and social impact assessments. To ensure the cycle remains unbroken, we have created a subsidiary company – InoBat Recycling. The same rigorous standards are deployed at every stage between raw material procurement and product end-of-life to ensure our pursuit of net-zero never wavers.

InoBat Auto production sites are designed with maximum attention to the environment and biodiversity. Recycled and renewable materials are prioritised during production, as is the use of renewable energy not just within our factories but within our supply, transportation, and delivery network.

There is no greater driver of innovation than necessity. Transitioning from fossil fuel to electric-powered mobility is vital to protect the future of our planet and humankind. InoBat Auto was born to help meet that challenge.
